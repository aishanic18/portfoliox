About

I'm a Ph.D. student in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I came to my doctorate through an interdisciplinary M.S. in Design Science at Michigan, which combined coursework across Design, Engineering, and Business — a perspective that continues to shape how I approach problems at the intersection of engineering, design, and sustainability.

Alongside my research, I work as an Investment Associate at Michigan Climate Venture, where I help evaluate and support early-stage climate technology. My earlier work spans research, teaching statistics and R programming, and leading a student engineering team.
